Set the strategy adopted by top level H-matrix/vector multiplication related functions, namely, vmult , Tvmult and Hvmult , as well as vmult_add , Tvmult_add and Hvmult_add .

When the strategy is task parallel, the two flags is_vmult and is_tvmult will be used to prepare necessary data on each thread.
