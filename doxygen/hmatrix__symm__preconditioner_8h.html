Detailed Description

Symmetric \(\mathcal{H}\)-matrix by inheriting from HMatrix, which is used as the preconditioner for SPD \(\mathcal{H}\)-matrices.

At the moment, its purpose is to wrap some member functions of HMatrix, such as HMatrix::vmult, which will be used as preconditioner in SolverCGGeneral.
