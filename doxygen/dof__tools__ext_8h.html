Build the topology for "DoF support point-to-cell" relation.

Note
2022-06-06 This topology is needed when the continuous finite element such as FE_Q is adopted. For the discontinuous finite element such as FE_DGQ, the DoFs in a cell are separated from those in other cells. Hence, such point-to-cell topology is not necessary.
